ACCOMMODATION AND HOUSING

The University of Dar es Salaam provides safe and affordable on-campus and off-campus housing for its students. The goal is to ensure a comfortable, inclusive, and supportive living environment that promotes academic success and community engagement.

Services Offered

    Accomodation service: Accommodation and housing services, including on-campus hostels and support for off-campus housing, Provision of on-campus student hostels for both male and female students.

    Support service:Support and guidance for students seeking off-campus accommodation. and Provision of basic utilities and services in hostels, such as water, electricity, and sanitation.

    Maintenance & Repairs:Maintenance and management of student residential facilities and ensure a safe and secure living environment within university residences.

Who Can Access This Service?

Undergraduate and postgraduate students, international students, and students with families are eligible for housing services. Staff may coordinate facility-related requests through Facilities Management; general residence life resources are primarily student-focused.
