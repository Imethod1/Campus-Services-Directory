LIBRARY SERVICES

OUR VISION MISSION AND CORE VALUES

VISION

To be a leading academic centre of excellence in the provision, dissemination and preservation of knowledge in the country.

MISSION

To provide contemporary information services that will empower the University in carrying out its core activities of teaching, learning, research, and community services/consultancy.

CORE VALUES

  • Quality Service: We value library service that is credible, responsible, and innovative, supporting teaching, learning, and lifelong learning.
  • Transparency: We value integrity and accountability in our interactions with colleagues, university members, and external communities.
  • Diversity: We value diversity in perspectives and cultures and provide open and inclusive access to information.
  • Collaboration: We value cooperation and teamwork in all aspects of our work, both within the University and with our partner institutions.

SERVICE OVERVIEW

The University Library supports teaching, learning, and research by provides access to physical and digital collections, borrowing services, interlibrary loan, research consultations, information literacy training, institutional repository access, and quiet and collaborative study spaces. Librarians partner with students and faculty to support teaching, learning, and research.

SERVICES OFFERED

  • Collections & Borrowing: Access to physical books, journals, e-books, databases, and interlibrary loan services.
  • Research Support & Consultations: One-on-one help with literature searches, citation management, and information literacy training.
  • Study Spaces & Resources: Quiet study areas, group project rooms, computer access, and collaborative learning facilities.
  • Institutional Repository: Access and deposit of university research outputs, theses, and scholarly publications.
  • Information Literacy Training: Workshops and guides on evaluating sources, database searching, and research skills.

WHO CAN ACCESS THIS SERVICE

Students, academic staff, and researchers have full access to collections and services. Alumni may have limited access to selected resources. External visitors can request day passes subject to library policies.
